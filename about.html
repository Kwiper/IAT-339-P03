About me

Hey! I'm Jonathan, a game designer, programmer, musician, whatever you need me to be. I aim to create interesting visual and interactive experiences through digital mediums!


I've been making games since I was 15, working on several game jams and mods with my friends and on my own, before pursuing game design as a career. I am currently studying Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University, with a concentration in Media Arts. I previously worked at Ludare Games as a junior game designer. I am also a member of SFU's game development club, where I've helped club leaders run workshops teaching people various aspects of game development!


When I'm not making games you can probably find me... playing games. Or making music. And being a student, I suppose...
